About Kyn

Built for skin like yours.

Kyn Skyn was created for people with fungus-prone skin. Conditions like seborrheic dermatitis, fungal acne, and Malassezia-related irritation show up differently on darker skin, yet are rarely represented in the skincare space. Most products on the market contain lipids that feed Malassezia, making things worse. Ours don't.

Our name is inspired by kin: a nod to connection, community, and care. Every formula and visual choice honors that ethos.

The Kyn. Made by us, for us.

because they have it.
